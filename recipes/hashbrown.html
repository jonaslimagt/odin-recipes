THE ODIN RECIPES

MONDSTADT HASH BROWN

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 large potato or 2 medium-sized potatoes
  • 2 pinecones
  • 4 c. oil
  • Rosemary, salt and pepper, for seasoning
  • Fruit jam, for accompaniment

DIRECTIONS

  1. Chop the potatoes in little cubes.
  2. Droil the potatoes until they become soft and then drain away the water.
  3. Melt the butter and infuse minced rosemary in it for ten minutes.
  4. Grind the pinecones and add them onto the potatoes.
  5. Season the mixture and mash up it until become a soft puree.
  6. Shape the hash browns into discs and freeze for 2 or 3 hours.
  7. Fry the hash browns in boiling oil and serve them with jam.
