THE ODIN RECIPES

KRABBY PATTY

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 Krabby Patty®™
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced cheese (American or Cheddar)
  • Hamburger buns
  • Ketchup, mayonnaise and mustard
  • Sliced pickle
  • Lettuce

DIRECTIONS

  1. Lay the Krabby Patty®™ on a grill for 5 minutes on each side.
  2. Lay the cheese on the Krabby Patty®™ and let melt for 2 minutes.
  3. Spread ketchup, mayonnaise and mustard on the buns.
  4. Lay the Krabby Patty®™ onto the buns.
  5. Add lettuce, onions and tomatoes.
