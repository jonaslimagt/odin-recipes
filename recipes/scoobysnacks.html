SCOOBY SNACKS

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 c. all purpose flour
  • 1 c. oatmeal
  • 1/2 c. sugar
  • 1/4 c. pound butter
  • 1/2 c. cocoa
  • 1/4 c. weed
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla extract

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix together all of the ingredients until combined.
  3. Bake for 8 minutes or until Scooby Snacks are set and golden brown.
