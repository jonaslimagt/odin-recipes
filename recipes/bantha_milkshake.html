THE ODIN RECIPES

BANTHA'S BLUE MLKSHAKE

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 c.) vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 c. bantha's blue milk
  • Whipped topping, for garnish
  • Sprinkles, for garnish
  • Endor's ewok cherry, for garnish

DIRECTIONS

  1. In a blender, blend together ice cream and bantha's blue milk on slow until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry, if desired.
